Taj mahal

The Taj Mahal is located in Agra, India. It is one of the Seven Wonders of the World. It was built by Shah Jahan in memory of Mumtaz Mahal. The white marble monument is a symbol of love and beauty.

Jaipur

Jaipur is known as the Pink City of India. It is famous for beautiful forts like Amer Fort and Hawa Mahal. The city has royal palaces and rich Rajasthani culture. Jaipur is full of colorful markets and traditional handicrafts.

Kerala

Kerala is known as “God’s Own Country.” It is famous for green backwaters, coconut trees, and calm nature. Houseboat rides in Alleppey are a major attraction. Kerala is peaceful and full of natural beauty.

Delhi

Delhi is the capital of India and a city full of history and modern life. It has famous places like Red Fort, India Gate, and Qutub Minar. The city is known for its busy streets, markets, and street food. Delhi shows a mix of old Mughal culture and new modern buildings.
